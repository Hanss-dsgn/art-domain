About Art Zone

Art Zone is a web-based virtual museum designed to make art accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world. Traditional museums require physical presence and have limited visiting hours, but Art Zone removes these barriers by bringing art directly to users through an online platform.

The platform showcases a wide range of artworks including paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital art. Users can explore different categories, view artwork details, and learn about the artists behind each masterpiece.

Our Objectives

  • Provide an accessible virtual museum experience
  • Showcase different categories of artworks
  • Organize artworks into digital galleries
  • Enhance user engagement through interactive design
